Though not an accessibility requirement, high contrast mode is a useful feature for users with low vision or color blindness. It can also be helpful in bright environments where screen glare makes it difficult to read standard text. This design pattern provides a way to toggle high contrast mode on and off, allowing users to choose the best viewing experience for their needs.

Required

  • All text must have a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 against the background.
  • The focus indicator must have a minimum contrast ratio of 3:1 against the background.

Best Practice

  • Focus states should use highly distinguishable styles such as an outline, border, or background change to ensure clarity in High Contrast Mode.
